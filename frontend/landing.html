Built by a practitioner,
for practitioners.

AI-SOC grew out of frustration with alert fatigue — spending hours triaging low-fidelity detections that should have been handled automatically. The goal was to build the SOC tool that a senior analyst would want, not the one a vendor would sell.

Every component was chosen for operational realism: the lab environment mirrors a real enterprise Active Directory domain, the detections were tuned against actual malware, and the LLM integration was built to assist — not replace — human judgment.

🎓 Background in offensive security and blue team operations — built with attacker mindset applied to defensive tooling.
🔬 All detections validated in an isolated Windows AD lab against real-world adversary tooling, not just simulated events.
🤝 Open to collaboration, feedback, and enterprise pilot engagements — reach out via LinkedIn or GitHub.
